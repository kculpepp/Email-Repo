The Affordable Care Act requires employers to provide you a 1095-C Form to document your enrollment in healthcare coverage. Weatherby Healthcare is currently producing a Form 1095-C for anyone who was employed by Weatherby Healthcare in 2015.

Your 1095-C will be mailed to your home address. Although you do not need to provide your 1095-C Form with your 2015 tax filing, please save this form with your tax documents.
