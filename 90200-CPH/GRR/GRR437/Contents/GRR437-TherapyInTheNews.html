Kalamazoo shooting victim leaves rehab.

On February 20, a mass shooting in Kalamazoo left six people dead and two wounded. One of the injured was 14-year-old Abigail Kopf. She sustained a severe head injury and was initially presumed dead at the scene.

Through Abigail’s determination and the support of an incredible staff at Mary Free Bed Rehabilitation Hospital, she is now able to walk, talk, and “almost run down the halls.”
