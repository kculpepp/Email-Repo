Hearing vest allows deaf individuals to feel speech.

Hearing loss affects 48 million people in the United States, and technological advances continue to provide new treatment options for those who seek it. One of these devices is a vest, which uses vibrating motors, that could allow deaf individuals to feel speech.

“The thing to note is that this is exactly what your inner ear does,” said David Eagleman, a co-creator of the vest. “Your inner ear just breaks things up into frequencies and you’ve got from high to low and that’s how your brain receives the information.”

While the vest is still in the development stage, it could eventually be an affordable alternative to current devices like cochlear implants.
