Lynchburg, VA

History is on full display in beautiful Lynchburg. And no visit is complete without exploring the city’s historic downtown district. You’ll find unique shops, historic sites, arts and culture venues, delicious restaurants, and more. It’s easy to see why so many people consider it a must-see destination.

Santa Barbara, CA

This gorgeous city offers visitors a relaxed coastal way of life. Sometimes referred to as “The American Riviera,” Santa Barbara’s golden hillsides cascade down into the ocean. This is where wine country, lush gardens, and festivities welcome you at every turn.

Rumford, ME

From skiing Black Mountain to having access to hiking trails galore, Rumford is paradise for those who love to spend time in nature. There are plenty of arts and entertainment options too, and just about any museum you can conjure up: art, history, children’s, railway, and more.
