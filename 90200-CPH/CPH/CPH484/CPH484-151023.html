Locum tenens helps you enjoy
practicing medicine again.

Meet Dr. O'Mara. He's a hard-working physician who enjoys helping his patients. He also chooses his schedule. Attending his kids' school events? No problem. Playing a round of golf with his buddies? Bring it on.

 
 

"The flexibility of locum tenens has allowed me to do that," he says. "I'm not bidden to a call schedule. When I got out of private practice I realized how much my life had been consumed by it."

For nearly a decade, Dr. O'Mara has made a career of taking locum tenens assignments. He handpicks opportunities with the most favorable schedules.

“I now have time with my family and I can’t describe what that’s worth. I do locum tenens because I love the freedom and the flexibility.”
