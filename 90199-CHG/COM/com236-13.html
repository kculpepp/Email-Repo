Lend a helping hand

Difficult and sometimes tragic events dramatically affect our people each year, which is why we established the Employee Compassion Fund as part of the Heart of CHG. The fund gives you the opportunity to donate money on a one-time or ongoing basis to help co-workers facing financial challenges. Since its launch, the program has benefitted numerous employees who say the assistance couldn’t have come at a better time.
