Having fun while giving back

Our core value of Putting People First extends beyond our walls and into the communities where we live and work. The Heart of CHG provides you the opportunity to give back to each other and to local communities through programs such as the United Way, Volunteer Time Off, Employee Network Groups and the Employee Compassion Fund.

Check out this CHG Pulse story to see the unique ways our people give back to the community and each other.
